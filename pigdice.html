Welcome to the game of Pig Dice!

Player info:







Instructions:
  • Players take turns to roll both dice. They can roll as many times as they want in one turn.
  • A player scores the sum of the two dice thrown and gradually reaches a higher score as they roll
  • The dice needs to be passed to account for score of that turn
  • If a single number '1' is thrown on either die, the score for that turn is lost and the turn ends. However a double '1' counts as a 25.
  • First player to 100 wins!
